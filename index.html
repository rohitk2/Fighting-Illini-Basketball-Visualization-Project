The FIghting Illini offense, doesn't seem to be a #12 team. They are #5 in points scored. However, their defense seems to be giving up a lot of points, because they are #3 in points given.
While I had said earlier that the Fighting Illini offense seems to be their strong point, they could stand to take better shots. They are #10 in Field Goal Percentage.
The Fighting Illini also need to improve their presence near the boards. They are also #12 in Rebound Margin being one of the 3 teams in the Big Ten with a negative boards ratio.
Now let's analyze their defense further. The Fighting Illini remarkably have the most steals per game. This can be attributed to our point guards himself, Trent Frazier who is #2 in steals in the Big-Ten, who will be continuing to compete next year, which is a good sign. However, the team's defense is relatively poor at blocking. The Fighting Illini are last in blocks per game.
